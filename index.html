Lee Chong Wei

Country: Malaysia 🇲🇾

Playstyle: Lightning-fast footwork, deceptive net play, sharp crosscourt smashes.

Legacy: World No.1 for 349 weeks; 3× Olympic silver; one of the greatest never to claim Worlds/Olympic gold.

Lin Dan

Country: China 🇨🇳

Playstyle: Explosive, aggressive, deceptive. Court dominator with clutch performances.

Legacy: Only man’s singles player with 2 Olympic golds & 5 World titles; the GOAT.

Lee Zii Jia

Country: Malaysia 🇲🇾

Playstyle: Offensive, powerful smashes, stylish backhand, high-risk/high-reward.

Legacy: 2021 All England champion; Malaysia’s next-gen leader after Chong Wei.

Kento Momota

Country: Japan 🇯🇵

Playstyle: Patient, tactical, pinpoint precision and rally control.

Legacy: 2× World Champion; dominated 2019 with 11 titles before his 2020 accident.

Victor Axelsen

Country: Denmark 🇩🇰

Playstyle: Physically dominant, big smashes, disciplined footwork.

Legacy: 2020 Olympic gold; 2× World Champ; now trains from Dubai HQ.
